Streetwearness?

Streetwearness was born in 2019 with the idea of bringing something innovative, offering the customer the top brands at an affordable price, finally being able to wear the latest fashion clothes without having to spend a fortune. In addition to the excellent price we also offer an excellent 1: 1 high quality product and all our products before being shipped are carefully checked by one of our specialized agents, and only after passing the checks are they shipped and delivered to the customer.

FAQ

Quality

All products are in excellent 1: 1 quality
Quality checks are carefully performed by our specialized agent on every single product before being packed and shipped.

Shipping

All orders placed during the week (excluding holidays) will be prepared within 7/8 working days.

Below you will find more information about shipments: 

FREE SHIPPING :

You will receive your order within 12-15 working days

Tracking number available.

To track the order we will send you the link with the Tracking Code, through which you can see the status of your order.

For more information you can always contact us on instagram or write an email to streetwearness@hotmail.com

Returns and Refunds

To be eligible for an exchange, your item must be in the same condition in which you received it. It must also be in the original packaging.

Gift cards cannot be returned.

To complete your return, we need an email receipt or other proof of purchase.

Reimbursement

After 24 hours from the purchase, a refund cannot be requested, you are entitled to a refund only if the shoes are delivered to you defective.

For items that are blocked by customs or are lost, we guarantee a new shipment of the same product at our expense.

Made
Once your return has been received and inspected, we will send you an email to notify you that we have received your returned item. We will also notify you of the approval or rejection of your return.

If you are approved, your replacement item will be processed and shipped as soon as it's ready.

Exchanges
We only replace items within 14 days if they arrive defective, damaged, or the wrong size. If you need to exchange it for the same item, please email us at Streewearness@hotmail.com


The shipping costs for returning the product are the responsibility of the user. Shipping charges are non-refundable.

Depending on your place of residence, the time it can take for the replaced product to reach you may vary.

It is recommended that you consider using a trackable shipping service or purchasing shipping insurance. We cannot guarantee that we will receive your returned item.
